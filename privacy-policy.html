Privacy Policy

At AAN Tourism, we take the privacy of our customers seriously and only collect information necessary for making bookings with us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard customer information.

Collection of Personal Information:

We gather your personal information mainly when you reach out to us to inquire about our products and services or make a booking. This typically includes your name, contact information, email address, physical address, credit card or payment information, travel requirements, and referral source.

Use of Information:

Any personal information collected from customers through our website will be used by AAN Tourism to process bookings, verify credit card information, and provide relevant information related to the customer’s travel or any additional services they may wish to receive.

Privacy of Your Information:

All information provided by our customers during online bookings is considered confidential and will not be shared or sold to any third party except for select suppliers essential for processing the customer’s order.

Legal Disclosure:

We may disclose personal information if we believe it is necessary to protect our company’s rights or comply with a court order or legal process.

Non-Personal Information:

AAN Tourism regularly gathers data such as website traffic, user behavior, and preferences to improve website performance. This data does not personally identify users.

Opt-Out:

We offer visitors the option to opt-out of receiving marketing emails or newsletters by using the unsubscribe link provided in such communications.

Contests and Surveys:

Participation in contests and surveys is voluntary, and users can choose not to provide personal information.

Secured Transaction:

We ensure that all transactions are carried out securely using encryption, secure socket layers, and firewalls to protect sensitive information like credit card details.
