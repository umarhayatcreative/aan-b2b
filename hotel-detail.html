Hotel Details:

The Apartments, Dubai World Trade Centre are located on Dubai’s Sheikh Zayed Road, next to Dubai International Convention & Exhibition Centre (DICEC). Spacious apartments in Block A offers free WiFi and free parking for the serviced apartments guests. Choose from a selection of one, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments in BLOCK A, all of which have an open-plan living and dining area. There is also a well-equipped kitchen with all necessary utensils. All guests can enjoy free access to ‘The Club ’ located in Block – C which offers many leisure facilities, including swimming pool , tennis court , a fitness center and an outdoor climbing wall. For children, there is a separate kids’ pool and play area. There is an international restaurant and pool bar located at The Apartments, Dubai World Trade Centre, Block C where the Continental breakfast will be served. Within walking distance of the property, guests will find a choice of restaurants, banks, and supermarket. Dubai’s Metro is right next to The Apartments. Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply. This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties. Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian. Please inform The Apartments, Dubai World Trade Centre in advance of your expected arrival time.
